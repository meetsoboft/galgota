Safety Tips & Precautions:

Your Safety is Our Top most Priority. Please Read and follow safety tips and Precautions to prevent any unforeseen unfortunate events.

  1. Better safe than sorry, Be Safe and Stay Alert, Know your surroundings, Don’t trust unknown people.
  2. Do not pay money for job offer. Most Recruiter/Employer never ask refundable money in exchange of job offer.
  3. Do not Share/Provide your personal and confidential information by phone/chat such as Bank account, Credit card, Debit card, Aadhar card, PAN card, OTP, Home address, Marital Status
  4. Never go alone for in-person interview , take family member or friend with you at interview location. Schedule your interview in a daytime during office hours only.
  5. Verify Recruiter/Employer and their work location or address before accepting job offer
  6. If you find Red flags during your communication with Recruiter/Employer then refuse job offer.
  7. Report and Block fraud people in chat function or contact us with details.
